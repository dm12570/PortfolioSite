Some Talents Part 2

Playing multiple instruments, Playing multiple instruments is apparently
a talent that most people have in their everyday life.

I have played, and am still playing, all sorts of instruments like the
trumpet. The trumpet is a nice brass instrument that requires a
very nice set of lungs.

The French Horn is another nice instrument to start out with.
Taking less lung capacity to play, the french horn is an excellent
choice to have a child start playing. I have been playing this since
seventh grade.

I also play the guitar, among many other instruments. The guitar
is a relatively light instrument that has a very nice sound when being played.
Unlike the violin, the guitar doesn't need a bow string to be played. You can use
a guitar pick or even just your fingers. Just make sure your hands are clean if you do.
