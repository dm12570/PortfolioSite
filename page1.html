Some Talents

I don't mean to brag, but I think I am pretty good at problem solving.
puzzle games that require problem solving are the games that I am best
at and keep me the most entertained.


The Rubiks Cube is one way that a person can show how good
they are at problem solving. How fast they can do it shows
how good they are at memorizing different things. I can solve
a rubiks cube in just about a minute.

A puzzle is another good way to test someone's problem solving skills.
If someone likes a puzzle, they like testing their brain on what the picture
looks like and then piecing it together.

Sudoku is another way to tell if someone likes problem solving.
I don't know why, the internet said it does and apparently the
internet doesn't lie to anyone... Nope... Never.
